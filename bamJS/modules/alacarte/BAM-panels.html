The Antiquity À-la-carte Application is an interactive digital atlas of the Ancient Mediterranean World. It is produced by Ancient World Mapping Center (AWMC) at The University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill.

It is designed, developed, and maintained by Ryan Horne, utilizing data from the AWMC produced by Richard Talbert, Jeffery Becker, Ryan Horne, Ray Belanger, Steve Burges, Luke Hagemann, Ashley Lee, and others.

Feature data is derived from Pleiades. River data is derived from Vmap0, Natural Earth, and the work of the AWMC. All other landscape data is originally derived from Vmap0 and produced by the AWMC following the Barrington Atlas. Map icons are partially derived from SJJB SVG Map Icons, Wikimedia Commons, MapServer, and the work of the AWMC.

All site content, map data, and maps created by the application are released under the Creative Commons CC BY-NC 3.0 license and are completely free for non-commercial use. The underlying javascript code is released under GPLv2.
