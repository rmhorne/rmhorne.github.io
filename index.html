This is the personal website of Ryan Horne, a digital humanities and information sciences specialist who focuses on planning, managing, and preserving interdisciplinary digital humanities research projects. As a Research Consultant in the GIS, Visualization, XR & Modeling team at the Office of Advanced Research Computing at UCLA, I collaborate with campus researchers at all levels of their digital scholarship, including conceptualization, implementation, and publication.
