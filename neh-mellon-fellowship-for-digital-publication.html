NEH-Mellon Fellowship for Digital Publication

  • Wed 20 December 2017
  • Posts
  • Ryan Horne
  • #NEH, #Mellon, #social-networks, #numismatics, #hellenistic-history

I am pleased to announce that I received a NEH-Mellon Fellowship for Digital Publication to continue my work on digital die studies and Aeolian coin networks in the Hellenistic era. You can find a placeholder page for the project at http://aeolian-coins.org. The project's code will be hosted at https://github.com/Aeolian-Alexanders.
